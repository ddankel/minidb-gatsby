## Recipes

Cloak:

- Base P3 Menoth White Base
- Highlight w/ VMC White
- Shade/smooth Menoth White Base
- Mix in DC Dark Burnt Umber for deepest recesses

Armor:

- Base GW Chainmail
- Wash GW Badab Black
- For stains, mix P3 Blue Ink and Yellow Paint 2:1.  Thin + wash/stain
